Sketching the portrait of her worldview

Luz Anderfelt's ultimate belief in people's potential to learn from their experiences has produced a kaleidoscopic life journey. A genuine
interest in science combined with drawing, music, and dance as well as playing team sports were the first activities of her formative years!
Learning a second language, making a family, brief travelling and some work experience prior to university life gave Luz a foundation for reading
Psychology and Organisations. She trained as a psychotherapist with an emphasis in philosophy, knowledge whe used in organisational and family
settings.

A glance of 'who Luz is'

Luz describes herself as 'I am an eagle when learning! On a helicopter ride when working 'organisationally!' Sailing when designing a task! In all,
Luz has a genuine interest in people across cultures. She likes to think that she is a realist and has a firm believe that people's capacity to do
great things depends on what they believe about themselves, and how they choose to portray it!
