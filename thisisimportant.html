My Hobies and Passions





A creative self navigating the world of coding! A Beginner's Experience!

Python, HTML, CSS, Responsive Websites, flexbox, lists, anchor tags, clickable link, organic design,
open source, to name a few, were part of my recent experience! These new language expressions emerged from
the recent course I took on language programing! Learning to speak French now is not only a passion but also
a need since I am relocating in France.

Art History has always been my passion so I constantly take courses on Art Appreciation. Photography, history
and writing, my language learning journey begins with an exploration of the french regions! I am currently
doing pilgrimage in the context of "learning as you Go". As I feel like a child learning french as a third
language, working with children has always been my passion as we explore together learning to see whilst doing
some life drawing and engage with knowledge about colour.

In a mentor's role

I find inspiration in the work mentees do. I currently do mentoring for studens in their last year of their degree
whilst in the process of thinking about what to write their dissertations about. Learning to have access to a second
language offers a platform to think and do things differently!

In a relaxing mood

I do enjoy walking and hiking, and love walking in the rain. I like to be 'fit'. I like to seek defining moments
to be in awe?. I love pursuing the extraordinary for the sake of human kind! something I find in the world
of the Arts, and in the history of any country's heroes: Birgitta of Sweden, Alma Deutscher, David Attenborough,
Jordan Peterson, Bethoven, Alain de Bottom, to name a few!
